RoadCommand LLC respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data. We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with advertisers. Full stop.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use RoadCommand:

  • Account information — name, email address, phone number
  • Payment information — processed securely through Stripe, we do not store card details
  • GPS location data — used to calculate deadhead miles and identify your region for fuel prices and weather alerts. Location data is processed on your device and is not stored on our servers.
  • Usage data — features used, loads viewed, loads booked. Used to improve the product.
  • Invoice and load records you enter manually

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the RoadCommand platform
  • To calculate GPS-based deadhead and fuel costs
  • To send account-related communications
  • To process your subscription payments
  • To respond to your support requests

We do not use your information for advertising. We do not sell your information to any third party. We do not share your route data, load history, or business information with any external parties without your explicit consent.

GPS and Location Data

RoadCommand requests access to your device GPS to calculate deadhead miles from your current position to load pickup locations and to identify your region for live fuel prices and weather alerts. This data is used in real time on your device. We do not log, store, or transmit your precise GPS coordinates to our servers.

You may deny GPS access at any time in your device settings. The platform will function without GPS but deadhead calculations will require manual entry.
