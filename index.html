What It Does

Everything Your Dispatcher Does. Plus AI.

RoadCommand gives you real net profit on every load, finds your best return before you deliver, tracks every invoice and broker, exports your tax records with BOL attachments, and generates word-for-word negotiation scripts — all from your phone.

🤖
AI Morning Briefing
Every morning RoadCommand tells you your outstanding invoices, overdue payments, maintenance coming due, and one tactical recommendation — personalized to your actual data.
📍
GPS Deadhead Calculator
Automatically calculates empty miles from your GPS position to the pickup city and factors that fuel cost into your real net profit — using your actual empty MPG, not loaded.
💰
Real Net Profit Display
Every load shows your actual take-home after fuel, deadhead, and operating costs. Not the gross rate. The number that hits your bank account.
🤝
AI Negotiation Scripts
Enter the broker offer, the lane, and the miles. RoadCommand generates a word-for-word phone script using live market rates and the broker payment history. Read it on the phone and get more money.
🔄
Return Load Finder
The moment you book a load, RoadCommand finds your best return loads from the delivery city ranked by round-trip net profit — matched to your actual HOS arrival time.
🏦
Broker Vault
Your complete broker network with payment history, MC numbers, contacts, and invoice history per broker. Search, filter by activity, and see exactly who owes you money at a glance.
📦
Tax Export with Attachments
Export any month or full year as a ZIP — every invoice gets its own folder with the summary, BOL photo, and Rate Confirmation attached. Hand your accountant one file.
⛽
Crowdsourced Diesel Prices
Real pump prices reported by RoadCommand drivers within 100 miles of your location — more accurate than EIA regional averages. Report what you paid and help every driver in your area.
🌤️
Live NWS Road Alerts
Live National Weather Service alerts filtered for truckers — chain requirements, high wind warnings, blizzard advisories, mountain pass closures — based on your GPS location.
🕐
HOS Arrival Calculator
Calculates your real arrival time using full FMCSA rules — 11 hour drive limit, 14 hour window, 10 hour reset, 30 minute break. Not just miles divided by speed.
📋
Invoice Tracker
Track outstanding and overdue invoices with color-coded status, one-tap broker calls, and a collapsible paid history. Upload BOL and Rate Con photos directly to each invoice.
🔩
Maintenance Tracker
Track every service interval with progress bars, cost per mile calculations, and due-soon alerts. Your true operating cost built into every profit calculation automatically.
